## 2. Scope — website and mobile apps

The Service includes:

- Our public website at **theoutreachproject.app**
- Native **iOS** and **Android** applications distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play

Our mobile apps use a **Capacitor** native shell that loads our production web application inside a secure in-app browser (WebView) at **https://theoutreachproject.app**. For most features, the data practices described in this policy are the same whether you use a web browser or our mobile apps, because both access the same hosted application and APIs.

This policy does **not** apply to third-party websites, nonprofits, sponsors, or services linked from TOP (for example, an external organization’s site). Those parties have their own privacy practices.

## 3. Information we collect

We collect information in the following categories:

### 3.1 Information you provide

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Account & profile** | Name, email address, display name, profile photo, onboarding answers (such as identity segment or affiliation you choose to share), bio, links | Create and maintain your account; personalize your experience |
| **User-generated content** | Community posts, comments, images you upload, application forms (sponsor, podcast guest, trusted resource, contact) | Operate community and intake features you choose to use |
| **Saved items** | Saved nonprofits, favorites, bookmarks | Sync preferences across devices when signed in |
| **Communications** | Messages sent through our contact form or support email | Respond to you |
| **Membership choices** | Tier selection, billing-related status (not full card numbers) | Provide optional paid membership benefits |

Account sign-in and authentication are handled by **WorkOS** (AuthKit). When you sign in, WorkOS processes your email and authentication credentials according to their policies and our instructions.

### 3.2 Payment information

Optional **membership** and related paid offerings are processed by **Stripe, Inc.** When you checkout, Stripe collects payment method details (such as card information) directly. **We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.** We receive and store billing-related references and status from Stripe (for example, customer ID, subscription status, invoice metadata) to operate your membership.

**Important for mobile users:** Membership purchases in the mobile apps are completed through **Stripe Checkout on the web** (in-app browser or Safari/Chrome flow). We do **not** use Apple In-App Purchase or Google Play Billing for these membership tiers at this time.

### 3.3 Information collected automatically

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Technical & log data** | IP address, device/browser or in-app WebView user agent, timestamps, request paths, error logs | Security, abuse prevention, reliability, debugging |
| **Session & cookies** | Authentication session cookies and similar tokens (via WorkOS and our hosting stack) | Keep you signed in; protect your account |
| **Usage within the app** | Pages or features you access while signed in (as reflected in server logs and application data) | Operate and improve the Service |

We do **not** use the mobile apps to collect precise geolocation, advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) for cross-app tracking, or contact lists from your device.

### 3.4 Device permissions (mobile apps only)

The mobile apps may request access **only when you choose** to upload a photo (for example, profile avatar or community post image):

- **Photo library** (iOS / Android): to select an existing image  
- **Camera** (iOS / Android): to capture a new image  

If you deny permission, you can still use most of the Service; you may need to upload images another way or skip image features.

We do not access your photo library or camera in the background.

## 4. How we use information

We use information to:

1. Provide, maintain, and secure the Service (sign-in, profiles, directory search, trusted resources, sponsors, podcasts, community, notifications, and membership features).
2. Process optional payments and maintain subscription status through Stripe.
3. Moderate community content and enforce our Terms of Use.
4. Respond to contact messages, applications, and support requests.
5. Send **service-related** communications (for example, account or billing notices). We do not sell your personal information for third-party advertising.
6. Analyze aggregated or de-identified usage to improve reliability and product design (we do not operate third-party ad networks in the app).
7. Comply with law, respond to lawful requests, and protect rights, safety, and integrity of users and the platform.

## 9. Your privacy rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- **Correct** inaccurate profile information (also available in-app)

**How to exercise rights:** Email [support@theoutreachproject.app](mailto:support@theoutreachproject.app) with the subject line “Privacy Request,” describe your request, and include the email address associated with your account. We may verify your identity before responding.

**Stripe billing:** Manage payment methods and subscriptions through the **Stripe Customer Portal** when available from your profile or billing settings.
